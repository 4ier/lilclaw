LilClaw Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Overview

LilClaw is a local-first AI assistant app. Your privacy is our top priority. LilClaw does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us (the developer).

Data Collection

LilClaw does not collect any user data. All conversations and settings are stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

LilClaw connects to AI API providers (such as OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, DeepSeek, etc.) that you configure with your own API key. Your conversations are sent directly from your device to these providers. We do not act as an intermediary and have no access to these communications. Please review the privacy policies of the AI providers you choose to use.

Data Storage

All app data, including your conversations and configuration, is stored locally on your Android device. No data is synced to any cloud service operated by us.

Analytics & Advertising

LilClaw does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, or advertising frameworks.

Children's Privacy

LilClaw is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
